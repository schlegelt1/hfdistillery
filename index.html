Est. 2014 - 2023

Our Story

Honeoye Falls Distillery was a small-batch craft distillery founded with a passion for quality spirits and traditional methods. For several years we proudly produced whiskey, gin, and other spirits in Honeoye Falls, New York.

Though the distillery has now closed its doors, the memories, friendships, and love for the craft remain. Thank you to all our customers, supporters, and community members.

Here's to the journey.
